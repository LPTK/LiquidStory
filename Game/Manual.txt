	Liquid Story - MANUAL.

The aim is to reach the space. Build the highest tower possible, but be careful! If the tower collapses, you will lose the game!
You may use ice particles to strengthen your structure and vapor particles to support it. Find these particles in the air, by reaching the bonuses.

CONTROLS:

Left Click		Create Particle

Middle Click/Escape	Pause/Unpause

Right Click		Change element

Mouse Wheel		Move camera up/down

Enter			Toggle fullScreen mode on/off

² (Square Key)		Show/Hide console
